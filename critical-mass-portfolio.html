Investment Criteria

How we underwrite.

A consistent framework for stage, check size, geography, and posture. We say no often so we can show up fully when we say yes.

/ Stage Seed → Series B Concentrated commitments to companies with technical conviction and credible commercial path.
/ Check Size $1M – $10M Lead and meaningful follow-on capacity through Series B, with selective continuation thereafter.
/ Geography U.S. & Allied Domestic and allied jurisdictions with clear export, security, and dual-use eligibility.
/ Posture Long-Hold, Active Operating partnership with founders. Board-engaged, technically literate, patient capital.
